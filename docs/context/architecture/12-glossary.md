# 12. Glossary

- Card: one of digit card, colored special card, or wildcard.
- Top card: current visible card on discard pile that constrains legal discards.
- Legal discard: a card matching color, digit, or special symbol, or a wildcard.
- Discarder: strategy function that selects a playable hand card.
- Turn direction: index movement through players in Game.play().
- Dealing: initial setup of draw pile, discard pile, and player hands.
